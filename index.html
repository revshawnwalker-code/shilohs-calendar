About Us

Shiloh Baptist Church is a welcoming congregation in Scranton, Pennsylvania, dedicated to worship, Bible study, and serving our community. Whether you're new to faith or have walked with the Lord for years, there's a place for you here.

Our pastor, Rev. Shawn Walker, leads our congregation in weekly worship, Sunday School, and Wednesday Bible Study. We'd love for you to join us, in person or online.
