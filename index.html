How to subscribe on your device
  • iPhone/iPad: Tap "Subscribe" above, then confirm under Settings → Calendar → Accounts.
  • Google Calendar (desktop): Other calendars → + → "From URL" → paste the feed link below.
  • Outlook: Add calendar → Subscribe from web → paste the feed link below.

Feed link: https://revshawnwalker-code.github.io/shilohs-calendar/calendar.ics

Note: calendar apps check subscribed feeds for updates every few hours, not instantly — that's normal for every calendar subscription, not specific to this one.
